Concept-first AI/ML research notes, paper reviews, and small experiments.

This repository is my public research notebook for organizing and sharing notes across artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, NLP, LLMs, and applied AI experiments.

The goal is not to collect random resources. The goal is to write clear notes, connect concepts with original papers, and build small experiments where implementation helps understanding.

## Repo Map

| Section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| [`concepts/`](concepts/) | Concept notes that explain important AI/ML ideas clearly |
| [`papers/`](papers/) | Structured paper reviews with problem, method, results, strengths, and limitations |

## Writing Approach

I use this repo to turn AI/ML topics into structured notes that are easier to revisit, improve, and share.

Concept notes focus on clear explanations and connected learning flow.

Paper reviews focus on the research problem, method, results, strengths, and limitations.

Experiments are added when a small implementation can make the idea more concrete.

```text
concept -> paper -> experiment
```
